A Tea House Like No Other
Serving delicately curated fine teas since 1926

Tea is our passion. Lady's Choice is a delight for tea connoisseurs and tea lovers alike. Our thoughtfully curated collection of high-quality, loose leaf fine teas represent the diverse cultures and communities of the world – crafted by our dedicated master steepers and infusers. Our family owned tea house is full of charm and ecclectic elegance located in the midst of Isabella, the flourishing museum district of New York City. Enjoy a cup with us to delight your senses, refresh your spirits and stimulate your mind. Enjoy some treats while you're at it – we hope you enjoy your visit with us from first sip to the last drop.

Meet the Experts

Bianca

Bianca

×
Bianca leads the amazing team of tea experts at Lady's Choice. She is a world traveler, tea expert and author of over 30 tea books. To her, the tea is theory, experimentation and rooted in daily practice.
Jade

Jade

×
Jade is a member of the International Tea Masters Association. Flavor profile, complexity and aromatic notes is favorite topic of conversation. Drawing from her background in traditional eastern medicine and herbology, she uses her extensive knowledge and experience to personally select each tea in our carefully curated tea library.
Nora

Nora

×
Tea sommelier specializing in fine tea and tea ware, with particular interest in Chinese, Japanese and Taiwanese tea. Tea is and art form to her – she loves unlocking new flavors in your food by pairing it with tea.
Olivia

Olivia

×
As one of the principal tea taster, Olivia have a vast knowledge of teas, tea plantations and factories in many parts of the world. Passionate about tea and the fine arts alike, she provides a unique perspective and aeshetic as she teaches educational tea masterclass seminars around the museum district.
Sienna

Sienna has travelled and tasted her way around the world’s tea estates whilst working as a master tea buyer and inquisitive blender. She enhances her tea selection constantly and is enthusiastic about tea and food pairing.

She applies the idea of everything has a purpose when blending teas as well as her presentation of the tea. She is also curates the photgraphy, art and floral arrangement at Lady's Choice.
