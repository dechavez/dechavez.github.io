Tea is our passion. Lady's Choice is a delight for tea connoisseurs and tea lovers alike. Enjoy a cup with us to delight your senses, refresh your spirits and stimulate your mind. Enjoy some treats while you're at it – we hope you enjoy your visit with us from first sip to the last drop.

About Us

Lady's Choice has been serving delicately curated fine teas since 1926. This tea house originated in the humble home of Rita Vedan in Chicago, IL. Her interests in the art of tea brought her to partner up with fellow tea artisans in Chicago and New York. When Rita's children moved to New York in 1954 to pursure their careers, she too ventured there to grow the business with her New York contacts. After all, home is where her family is - as she used to say.

The Vedan family still oversees the operations for this tea house and invites the very best tea connoisseurs who reflect their same passion for tea in to the Lady's Choice Family.

Enjoy a unique experience with a perfect cup of tea crafted by our incredibly talented and knowledgable master steepers and infusers.
