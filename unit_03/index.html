The Beginner Creative Ninja Code

  • Thou Shall Observe Proper Kerning
  • Be Mindful of Readability/Legibility
  • Have Purposeful Hierarchy
  • Avoid Widows And Orphans (in typography - not the people - unless they mean you or others harm)
  • Master Consistency
  • Thou Shall Not Stretch Type
  • Embrace the White Space
  • Be Quick and Be Simple
  • Clean up thy code/markup
  • Be mindful of your audience and media when designing
  • Beware of Comic Sans and animated gifs
